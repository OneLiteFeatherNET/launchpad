## What is this?

The spawn lighthouse is a shared community project: everyone is welcome to help build. The goal is a recognisable landmark with atmosphere — not an enormous structure, but capped at 80 blocks on purpose so it still reads well from the nearby harbour.

## How can you help?

- Gather materials: quartz, prismarine, sea lanterns
- Interior decoration of the gallery: texts and banners for milestones
- Grab the schematic and rehearse the build on your plot first
