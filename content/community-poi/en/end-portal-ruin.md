## What is this?

The End portal ruin is the place to go for anyone willing to take the leap into the End. It sits on a small hill in the forest north of spawn and is signposted along a path with markers.

## Lore

A long-forgotten culture built a temple here — the forest has long since taken the building back. Deep inside, the activated portal waits for anyone brave enough to jump through.
