## What is this?

Reporters Without Borders opened a library in Minecraft in 2020 that gathers censored journalism from around the world. We rebuild the architecture as a tribute; the content is curated separately.

## Reference

Original project: [uncensoredlibrary.com](https://www.uncensoredlibrary.com/de){target=_blank rel=noopener}.

## How can you help?

- Research on censored texts that can later live as books inside the library
- Material farming for the main building (quartz, sandstone, premium detail blocks)
- Help with area preparation and finalising the origin survey
